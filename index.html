Who We Are

Innovating Silicon to Solution

At SilicWorks, we specialize in the meticulous execution of the physical implementation journey, true to our core philosophy of "Innovating Silicon to Solution." We bridge the critical gap between logical intent and physical reality by providing premier RTL-to-GDSII services alongside a robust portfolio of silicon-proven IP cores. Our expertise encompasses every stage of the backend flow—including synthesis, floorplanning, clock tree synthesis (CTS), and sign-off timing closure—ensuring that complex designs are optimized for power, performance, and area (PPA) at advanced process nodes. By integrating our high-performance Intellectual Property into a seamless design flow, we transform your RTL code into manufacture-ready GDSII files, delivering precision-engineered hardware solutions that empower the next generation of AI, automotive, and telecommunications technology.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What does “RTL-to-GDSII” mean?

RTL-to-GDSII represents the complete chip design journey — from writing RTL code (Verilog/SystemVerilog) to generating the final GDSII layout file ready for semiconductor fabrication (tape-out).

What industries does SilicWorks serve?

We support AI, Automotive, Industrial IoT, Telecommunications, and next-generation embedded systems requiring high-performance, low-power silicon solutions.

Do you provide silicon-proven IP cores?

Yes. We develop and deliver silicon-proven IPs including RISC-V cores, communication protocols (UART, SPI, I2C), AMBA bus protocols (APB, AHB, AXI), and essential system peripherals.

What makes SilicWorks different from other VLSI companies?

Unlike traditional service providers, we combine service expertise with in-house IP development. This enables deeper architectural understanding, seamless integration, and optimized PPA performance.

Do you offer VLSI training and internships?

Yes. We provide industry-focused training programs in RTL design, Physical Design, ASIC Verification, SoC protocols, and internships with hands-on project experience.

How can companies collaborate with SilicWorks?

Organizations can partner with us for custom ASIC development, IP licensing, physical design support, training collaborations, and complete end-to-end semiconductor solutions.

What services does SilicWorks provide?

SilicWorks offers end-to-end RTL-to-GDSII semiconductor design services, including RTL design & verification, physical design, DFT services, and silicon-proven IP development.
